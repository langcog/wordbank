Thank you to all of the generous researchers who have contributed data to Wordbank!

All uses of Wordbank in published papers should cite:
Frank, M. C., Braginsky, M., Yurovsky, D., & Marchman, V. A. (in press). Wordbank: An open repository for developmental vocabulary data. Journal of Child Language.

In addition, researchers should cite those datasets that they use, based on this page (if there is no linked citation on this page, no citation is necessary). If researchers use more than five datasets, there is no need to cite each individual dataset. As a special case of this rule, users of the English data need only cite the Wordbank database.

To contribute your data, please email wordbank-contact@stanford.edu.
