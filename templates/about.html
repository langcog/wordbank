Wordbank is an open database for storing information about children's vocabulary growth.

Wordbank archives data from the MacArthur-Bates Communicative Development Inventory (MB-CDI), a family of parent-report questionnaires. Do you have data you would like to contribute?

Wordbank enables researchers to recover data filtered by source, age, gender, word, and a host of other filters, enabling simple export of plain-text data for further analysis.

Wordbank also include a number of reports based on recent research on children’s vocabulary: see how children’s vocabulary grows and changes across early childhood.

Wordbank is approved by the MB-CDI avisory board, and is a project of the Stanford Language and Cognition Lab, PI Michael C. Frank.
